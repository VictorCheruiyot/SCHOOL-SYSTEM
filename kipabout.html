Kio Secondary School was founded in 2003 by the parents of Kio, Githima & Taragonik. The first students sat for kcse in 2009. The school is located in kio village, kipkewa sublocation, Kuresoi location, Kuresoi division, Kuresoi subcounty, Nakuru county. It is a mixed Day and Boarding school. The school boasts a serene environment with a tea plantation background. It is located 5 minutes drive from Chepsir-Kiambereria highway.
